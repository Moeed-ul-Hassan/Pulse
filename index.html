Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Pulse HMS

Can Pulse HMS work without internet?

Yes! Pulse HMS features offline booking cache and sync functionality. Your clinic can continue operating even when Wi-Fi fails. All data is stored locally and syncs automatically when the connection is restored.

Offline Mode Available

Is Pulse HMS suitable for small clinics or solo doctors?

Absolutely! Unlike overbuilt ERP systems designed for large hospitals, Pulse HMS is specifically built for small clinics and solo practitioners. You get all the features you need without the complexity you don't.

Perfect for Small Clinics

How does the patient dashboard work?

No complicated patient logins required! Patients access their dashboard using a simple secret code. This eliminates the hassle of remembering passwords while keeping patient information secure and easily accessible.

Secret Code Access

How is Pulse HMS different from other HMS tools?

Pulse HMS stands out with AI-powered appointment summaries, QR code check-ins, Fake Data Mode for safe testing, and intuitive design. We focus on what small clinics actually need, not enterprise bloat.

AI-Powered Features

Can my staff learn this without training?

Yes! Most staff members can learn Pulse HMS in just 15 minutes. The interface is designed to be intuitive and user-friendly, eliminating the need for extensive training sessions.

15-Minute Learning Curve

How do appointments and check-ins work?

It's incredibly simple: Scan the QR code, check-in. That's it! No complicated processes or multiple steps. Patients can check themselves in, reducing wait times and staff workload.

QR Code Check-ins

Is my patient data safe?

Your patient data is protected like a vault. We use local data storage, advanced encryption practices, and give you complete offline privacy control. Your data never leaves your clinic unless you choose to sync.

Bank-Level Security

Can I try it with dummy data first?

Absolutely! Use our Fake Data Mode to explore all features without any risk. Test appointments, patient management, and reporting with safe dummy data before going live with real patient information.

Risk-Free Testing

Does it support custom branding and reports?

Yes! Add your clinic's logo, customize report layouts, and create branded mini reports. All printouts and digital reports reflect your clinic's professional branding.

Custom Branding

What's the pricing?

Pulse HMS is built for your budget without compromise. We offer transparent pricing tiers that beat bloated enterprise solutions. Get enterprise-level features at small clinic prices.

Transparent Pricing
